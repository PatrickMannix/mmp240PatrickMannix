Great Pizza Experience

This is a great Pizza pie recipe for those who want to part take amd particpate in making your own Pizza, to experience your own hands in the Pizza making process

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Can go with or without toppings of your choosing
  • A oven is needed

Directions

    1. To handle the dough you want place it on a cutting board. 2. Then to have a roller and a cutting roller on next to the cuttung board. 3. For turning the Dough into a Pizza outline you want use hands and/or roller to flat out and roll up the dough into the crust being baked. 4. After prepping thr dough just right you want to put sauce on the dough, preferably with a ladel if you have one. 5. Then you put the cheese on top of the sauce and spread (the amount of cheese used determines if you want extra cheese on your pizza or not). 6. Once all set in done you want to put the Pizza into the oven, set the ferinhiet to 950 degrees to bake ur Pizza under 1 minute or 60 seconds. 7. The minute is up on the oven and your Pizza is all done.
